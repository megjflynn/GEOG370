WGS84 Projection

In this projection the sizing and area of the continents are accurate for the most part, however, the shape is not. There is distortion in the shape at the poles.

Aitoff Projection

This projection has distortions in both size and shape. Area appears larger at the poles and at the far left and right edges of the map. The shape also becomes distored as you move away from the center.

Psuedo Mercator Projection

The shape is accurate in this projection however the size becomes distored as you move away from the equator towards the poles.

Sphere Winkel Projection

This map projection displays minor distortions in shape and size. Towards the poles, area is distored slightly large and more ellipsoid than spherical.

World Cylindrical Equal Area Projection

Size appears accurate in this projection but shape is distored significantly. At the poles the shapes are stretched significantly.

World Equidistant Conic Projection

This projection changes both shape and size of the map as you go from the center outward. The center of the map is very spherical and as you go towards the edge it becomes large and more of an ellipse.

North Pole Azimuthal Equidistant Projection

This projection is similar to the last. As you move away from the center the circles are stretched into ellipes and are made larger.

Africa Sinusoidal Projection

In this map, shape is distorted. In contrast to previous maps, as you move toward the poles the circles are strecthed virtically rather than horizontally.

World Van der Grinten I Projection

This projection preserves shape well but the sizing is slighlty distorted as it moves towards the poles.
